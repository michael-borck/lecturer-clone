# LecturerClone - Product Specification
## Your expertise, everywhere

### Executive Summary

LecturerClone is an AI-powered service that enables educators to create personalized video lectures by uploading photos, scripts, and voice samples. The system generates realistic videos of lecturers delivering custom content in their own voice and appearance, allowing them to scale their teaching impact without being physically present.

### Product Vision

Transform education by democratizing high-quality lecture creation, enabling any educator to produce professional video content that maintains their personal teaching presence while scaling to unlimited audiences.

### Core Value Proposition

- **For Individual Lecturers**: Create unlimited video content without recording sessions
- **For Educational Institutions**: Scale faculty expertise across multiple courses and campuses
- **For Content Creators**: Generate educational content with consistent presenter quality

### Target Market

**Primary:**
- University professors and lecturers
- Online course creators
- Corporate training departments
- Educational content companies

**Secondary:**
- K-12 educators
- Tutorial creators
- Language instructors

### Key Features

#### Core Functionality
- **Streamlined Video Input**: Single lecture video with matching script for one-time setup
- **Intelligent Frame Extraction**: Automatic keyframe selection and lecturer-focused cropping
- **AI-Generated Lectures**: Full video creation with synchronized lip movements
- **Voice Cloning**: Personalized text-to-speech extracted from source video
- **Visual Consistency**: Maintains lecturer appearance across videos
- **Quality Control**: Multiple generation attempts with automated scoring
- **Model Persistence**: Train once, generate unlimited content with script-only workflow

#### User Experience
- **One-Time Video Setup**: Upload single lecture video with script for model training
- **Automatic Frame Processing**: Intelligent cropping and enhancement to focus on lecturer
- **Script-to-Video Generation**: Create new content using only text input after initial setup
- **Real-time Progress Tracking**: Live updates during processing and training
- **Preview & Approval Workflow**: Review before final generation
- **Model Management**: Update or retrain personal models as needed
- **Template Library**: Pre-built educational formats

#### Quality Features
- **Intelligent Frame Selection**: Automatic extraction of diverse, high-quality training frames
- **Lecturer-Focused Preprocessing**: Smart cropping and enhancement to optimize face visibility
- **Multi-Generation Strategy**: 3-5 attempts per segment for optimal results
- **Automated Quality Scoring**: Face consistency, lip sync accuracy, audio clarity
- **Segmented Processing**: Break long scripts into manageable chunks
- **HD Upscaling**: Professional video output quality
- **Frame Interpolation**: Smooth motion and transitions
- **Model Versioning**: Track and update personal AI models over time

### Product Tiers

#### Free Tier - "Starter"
- 1 video per month (max 2 minutes)
- Single generation attempt
- Standard quality (720p)
- Basic templates
- Community support

#### Basic Tier - "Educator" ($29/month)
- 10 videos per month (max 5 minutes each)
- 3 generation attempts per video
- HD quality (1080p)
- Advanced templates
- Email support
- Batch processing

#### Premium Tier - "Institution" ($99/month)
- Unlimited videos (max 15 minutes each)
- 5+ generation attempts
- 4K quality option
- Custom templates
- Priority processing
- Phone support
- API access
- White-label options

#### Enterprise Tier - "Custom"
- Custom limits and features
- On-premise deployment options
- Advanced analytics
- Dedicated account management
- Custom integrations
- SLA guarantees

### Risk Assessment

**Technical Risks:**
- AI model reliability and consistency
- Processing time scalability
- Copyright and deepfake concerns
- Hardware/infrastructure costs

**Market Risks:**
- Educational institution budget cycles
- Competitor advancement
- Regulatory changes around AI-generated content
- User acceptance of AI-generated lectures

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Transparent AI disclosure requirements
- Robust terms of service and usage policies
- Diversified technology stack with fallback options
- Strong focus on educational use cases and ethics
